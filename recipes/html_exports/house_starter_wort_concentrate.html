House Starter Wort Concentrate

Jar size: 12 oz mason jars Fill per jar: 10 oz concentrate Batch size: 8 jars Concentrate gravity: 1.135 Reconstituted starter: 1.0 L at ~1.040 Dilution per jar: add 23.8 oz (704 mL) RO water Hops / bitterness: none

Grains

  • 3.40 lb (1.54 kg) Pale Malt
  • 0.10 lb (45 g) Light Munich Malt (optional; omit for a cleaner pale wort)

Yeast

  • None in the canned concentrate

Hops

  • No hops

Mash Schedule

  1. Mash at 149.0-150.0F (65.0-65.6C) for 60 minutes.
  2. Mash out at 168.0F (75.6C) for 10 minutes.

Fermentation Schedule

Fermentation Equipment: Grainfather GF30 Pro Conical + Grainfather Glycol Chiller

  1. Add 23.8 oz (704 mL) boiled-and-cooled RO water.
  2. Optional: add a small dose of standard beer yeast nutrient.
  3. Aerate well, then pitch yeast.
