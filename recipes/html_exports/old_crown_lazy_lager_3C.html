Old Crown Lazy Lager - BJCP 3C Czech Premium Pale Lager

Batch size (into fermenter): 5.0 gal (19.0 L) OG: 1.052-1.053 FG: 1.012-1.014 ABV: 5.0-5.4% IBU: 32-35 SRM: 3.8-4.3 Carbonation: 2.4-2.6 vols Mash pH (room temp): 5.30-5.40

Grains

  • Bohemian Pilsner Malt: 9.40 lb (4.26 kg)
  • Munich II: 0.50 lb (0.23 kg)

Yeast

  • Jasper Yeast `JY001 Czech Lager 1`, fresh and healthy, preferred
  • A true Czech lager strain in the WY2278 / WLP802 class
  • ~350B cells for 5.0 gal (19.0 L) at OG 1.052-1.053

Hops

  • Whirlfloc: 1 tablet at 15 min

Mash Schedule

  1. 168.0F (75.6C) for 10 min mash out

Fermentation Schedule

Fermentation Equipment: Grainfather GF30 Pro Conical + Grainfather Glycol Chiller

  1. Chill to 48.0-50.0F (8.9-10.0C), oxygenate once at pitch to ~8-10 ppm dissolved O2.
  2. Hold 50.0F (10.0C) through active primary until ~50-60% apparent attenuation, not by fixed day count.
  3. Raise to 58.0-60.0F (14.4-15.6C) and hold until terminal gravity is stable for 2 days.
  4. Run warm forced VDK check before crash.
  5. Crash to 42.0F (5.6C) after clean VDK.
  6. Lager at 42.0F (5.6C) for 3-4 weeks minimum on the verified GF30 floor.
