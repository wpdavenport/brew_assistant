Davenport ESB - BJCP 11C Strong Bitter

OG: 1.057 FG: 1.013 ABV: 5.8% IBU: ~37 Color: 11 SRM Mash pH (room temp): 5.30

Grains

  • 9.72 lb (4.41 kg) Simpsons Pale Malt
  • 0.76 lb (0.34 kg) Simpsons Crystal Malt
  • 0.02 lb (0.01 kg) Chocolate Malt

Yeast

  • Wyeast 1968 London ESB Ale (G1 repitch from fridge slurry) or WLP002 equivalent

Hops

  • 0.70 oz (20 g) Target (9.6% AA) - 60 min
  • 0.30 oz (9 g) Northdown (7.3% AA) - 20 min
  • 0.30 oz (9 g) Challenger (7.75% AA assumed midpoint from 6.5-9.0% bag range) - 20 min
  • 0.35 oz (10 g) East Kent Goldings (6.1% AA) - 10 min
  • 0.35 oz (10 g) East Kent Goldings (6.1% AA) - flameout, 10 min steep
  • Whirlfloc: 1 tablet at 10 min

Mash Schedule

  1. Mash 60 minutes at 151.0 F (66.1 C).
  2. Mash out 10 minutes at 168.0 F (75.6 C).

Fermentation Schedule

Fermentation Equipment: Grainfather GF30 Pro Conical + Grainfather Glycol Chiller

  1. Day 0-2: hold 65.0 F (18.3 C).
  2. Day 3: raise to 67.0 F (19.4 C).
  3. Day 4-9: hold 68.0 F (20.0 C) through terminal gravity, VDK check, and crash decision.
  4. Crash to 42.0 F (5.6 C) for 48-72 hr only after stable terminal gravity and clean VDK.
