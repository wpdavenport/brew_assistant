Packaging (Keg)

Oxidation kills clone accuracy. 90 Minute is resinous and bright, not dulled.

  • Sanitize keg → fill w/ sanitizer → push out w/ CO2 at 5 psi (keg is now O2-free)
  • Closed transfer: liquid post to dump valve, push w/ 2–3 psi CO2. No splashing.
  • Seal keg. Purge headspace: pull PRV 3× at 15 psi
  • Set regulator 12–13 psi at 38°F (targets 2.3–2.5 vols CO2)
  • Carbonate 7–10 days before serving or evaluation
