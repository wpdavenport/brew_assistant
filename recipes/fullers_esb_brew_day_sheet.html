1. Water Prep

  • Measure 7.66 gal (29.0 L) RO water into kettle.
  • Add salts: 0.28 oz gypsum + 0.16 oz CaCl2 + 0.04 oz epsom. Stir fully.
  • Heat to mash-in strike temperature and verify before dough-in.

System note: If you do minimal basket pressing, keep 0.3-0.5 gal (1.1-1.9 L) hot RO on standby for pre-boil volume correction.
