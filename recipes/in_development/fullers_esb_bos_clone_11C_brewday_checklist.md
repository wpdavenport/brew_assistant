# Brew Day Checklist — Copper Crown ESB (BOS Fuller's Clone, 11C)

Use with `recipes/in_development/fullers_esb_bos_clone_11C.md`

## Targets
- OG: 1.057
- FG: 1.013
- ABV: 5.8%
- IBU: 37
- Mash pH: 5.30 (room-temp reading)
- Fermentation start temp: 64.4F (18.0C)

## Ingredients (quick pull)
- Grains:
  - 4.10 kg (9.04 lb) Maris Otter
  - 0.28 kg (0.62 lb) English Medium Crystal Malt (Thomas Fawcett)
  - 0.16 kg (0.35 lb) Amber Malt
  - 0.08 kg (0.18 lb) Torrified Wheat
- Sugar:
  - 0.16 kg (0.35 lb / 5.64 oz) Invert Sugar No.2 (10 min left in boil)
- Hops:
  - 25 g (0.88 oz) Target @ 60
  - 18 g (0.63 oz) Northdown @ 20
  - 15 g (0.53 oz) Challenger @ 10
  - 12 g (0.42 oz) Northdown @ flameout (10 min steep)
- Kettle fining:
  - 1 Whirlfloc tablet @ 10
- Yeast:
  - Wyeast 1968 / WLP002 (healthy pitch)

## Water and salts (RO build)
- Total liquor: ~29 L (7.66 gal)
- Target profile (ppm): Ca 110, Mg 5, Na 15, Cl 80, SO4 150, HCO3 40
- Starting guideline:
  - Gypsum 8.0 g (0.28 oz)
  - Calcium chloride 4.5 g (0.16 oz)
  - Epsom 1.0 g (0.04 oz)

## Timeline (brew day)
### T-24 to T-12 hr (night before)
- Build yeast starter (or verify fresh, adequately sized pitch).
- Mill grain.
- Weigh hops into labeled cups: 60 / 20 / 10 / flameout.
- Weigh salts and boil additions.
- Sanitize fermenter and transfer path.

### T-90 min
- Heat strike water.
- Add mash salts.
- Dough in and verify mash temp.

### T-85 min
- Start mash timer: 151.0F (66.1C) for 60 min.
- Recirculation on.

### T-25 min
- Raise to mash out: 168.0F (75.6C), hold 10 min.

### T-15 min
- Lift basket, drain thoroughly.
- Start heating to boil.

### Boil start (60 min total)
- 60 min: add 25 g (0.88 oz) Target.
- 20 min: add 18 g (0.63 oz) Northdown.
- 10 min: add 15 g (0.53 oz) Challenger + Whirlfloc + 0.16 kg (0.35 lb / 5.64 oz) invert sugar.
- 0 min: flameout, add 12 g (0.42 oz) Northdown, steep 10 min.

### Chill + transfer
- Chill to 64.4F (18.0C) quickly.
- Whirlpool/rest, then transfer clear wort.
- Take OG reading and record.
- Oxygenate once at pitch.
- Pitch yeast.

## Fermentation schedule (GF30 + glycol)
- Day 0-3: 64.4F (18.0C)
- Day 4-6: ramp to 67.1F (19.5C) and gently rouse yeast once on Day 4
- Day 7-9: hold 68.0F (20.0C) for cleanup
- Day 8-9: forced diacetyl test before crash decision
- Crash: 35-37F (2-3C) for 48-72 hr after stable FG and clean sensory

## Critical QC checkpoints
- Mash pH at 10-15 min: 5.30 room-temp.
- Pre-boil volume target: 6.3-6.8 gal (23.8-25.7 L).
- Pre-boil gravity target: 1.043-1.046 (temp-corrected; adjust with top-up water or boil length as needed).
- Post-boil volume (pre-chill) target: 5.2-5.5 gal (19.7-20.8 L).
- Post-boil gravity (pre-chill) target: 1.055-1.058 (temp-corrected).
- Into-fermenter volume and wort temp at pitch recorded.
- Post-boil OG near 1.057.
- Actual boil-off rate recorded for future water planning.
- Fermentation SG checkpoints logged (Day 3, Day 7, terminal FG with stable 48 hr).
- Before crash: pass forced diacetyl test and confirm no butter/butterscotch.
- Forced VDK result logged with date and initials.
- Packaging log complete: package date, FG at package, CO2 setting (PSI + temp), competition date.
- Packaging: closed transfer only.

## Traps to avoid
- Mash too warm -> sweet/heavy finish.
- Poor oxygen control post-pitch -> stale/muted malt-hop balance.
- Packaging before full cleanup -> diacetyl fault.
- Over-carbonating -> loses authentic ESB presentation.

## Packaging target
- Carbonation: 1.9-2.1 vols CO2.
- Best competition window: 3-6 weeks after packaging.
