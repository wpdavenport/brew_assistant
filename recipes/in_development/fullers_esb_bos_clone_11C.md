# Copper Crown ESB (BOS Fuller's Clone, BJCP 11C Strong Bitter)

## Intent
Competition-calibrated Fuller's ESB-style clone targeting high drinkability, layered English malt, and firm/smooth bitterness.

## Target Parameters
- Batch size: 5.0 gal (19 L)
- OG: 1.057
- FG: 1.013
- ABV: 5.8%
- IBU: 37
- SRM: 11
- Mash pH: 5.30 (room-temp reading)

## Grain Bill
- 4.10 kg (9.04 lb) Maris Otter Pale Malt
- 0.28 kg (0.62 lb) English Medium Crystal Malt (Thomas Fawcett)
- 0.16 kg (0.35 lb) UK Amber Malt
- 0.16 kg (0.35 lb / 5.64 oz) Invert Sugar No.2 (late boil)
- 0.08 kg (0.18 lb) Torrified Wheat

## Hop Schedule
- 25 g (0.88 oz) Target (11.0% AA) @ 60 min
- 18 g (0.63 oz) Northdown (8.5% AA) @ 20 min
- 15 g (0.53 oz) Challenger (7.0% AA) @ 10 min
- 12 g (0.42 oz) Northdown (8.5% AA) @ flameout, 10 min steep

## Yeast
- Wyeast 1968 London ESB Ale (or WLP002)
- Pitch healthy starter equivalent to ~200 billion cells for 19 L at 1.057

## Water (from RO)
Target profile (ppm):
- Ca 110
- Mg 5
- Na 15
- Cl 70
- SO4 150
- HCO3 40

Starting liquor guideline for ~29 L (7.66 gal) total water:
- Gypsum: 8.0 g (0.28 oz)
- Calcium chloride: 4.0 g (0.14 oz)
- Epsom salt: 1.0 g (0.04 oz)
- Acid adjust to mash pH 5.30

## Mash Program (G40 full-volume)
- 148.1F (64.5C) for 35 min
- 152.6F (67.0C) for 30 min
- 168.0F (75.6C) mash out for 10 min

## Boil
- 60 min vigorous boil
- Add invert sugar with 10 min remaining
- Add Whirlfloc at 10 min

## Fermentation Schedule (GF30 + glycol)
- Day 0-2: 64.4F (18.0C)
- Day 3-5: ramp to 67.1F (19.5C)
- Day 6-8: hold 68.0F (20.0C) for diacetyl cleanup
- Crash to 35-37F (2-3C) for 48-72 h after stable FG and clean sensory check

## Packaging
- Closed transfer only
- Target carbonation: 1.9-2.1 vols CO2
- Best judging window: 3-6 weeks post-package

## BOS Execution Notes
- Keep oxygen out after fermentation starts; this style loses brightness quickly when oxidized.
- Do not over-hop late; Fuller's profile is bitter-balanced, not modern hop-saturated.
- Keep yeast healthy and do not underpitch: this is where ester quality and diacetyl control are won.
- Sensory checkpoint before packaging: no butter/butterscotch, no harsh mineral bite, finish must be dry-balanced.
