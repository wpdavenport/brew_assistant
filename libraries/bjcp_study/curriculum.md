# BJCP Study Curriculum (Online Entrance Exam Focus)

## Goal
Prepare for the BJCP online entrance exam by building practical recall, exam speed, and fault diagnosis discipline.

## Track Order
1. BJCP structure and exam strategy
2. Ingredients and process fundamentals
3. Off-flavor diagnosis and prevention
4. BJCP styles and commercial examples
5. Judging mechanics and score calibration
6. Mock exam drills and remediation

## Session Formats
- `teach`: concise lesson + key memory anchors.
- `quiz`: short adaptive question sets (5-20).
- `mock`: timed mixed-domain exam blocks.
- `review missed`: targeted re-test of weak tags.

## Topic Map
- `exam_structure`: BJCP program, exam format, passing mindset.
- `ingredients`: malt, hops, yeast, water impacts.
- `process`: mash, boil, fermentation, packaging faults.
- `off_flavors`: DMS, diacetyl, acetaldehyde, phenols, oxidation, astringency, infection.
- `styles_core`: major style families and boundaries.
- `styles_comparison`: lookalike style differentiation.
- `judging_process`: aroma/flavor/mouthfeel/overall method and deduction discipline.

## Readiness Standard
- Overall quiz accuracy >= 80%
- Off-flavor tag accuracy >= 85%
- Style-comparison accuracy >= 80%
- Two consecutive mock blocks >= 80%
