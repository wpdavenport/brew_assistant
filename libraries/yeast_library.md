# House Yeast Library

## Chico (US-05 equivalent)
- Temp range: 60–72°F (16–22°C)
- Attenuation: 78–82%
- Flocculation: Medium
- Flavor: Clean, light citrus
- Pressure tolerance: Moderate (≤15 psi)

## English Ale (1968-type)
- Temp range: 64–70°F (18–21°C)
- Attenuation: 67–71%
- Flocculation: High
- Flavor: Fruity esters, malt accentuation
- Pressure tolerance: Low

## German Lager (34/70-type)
- Temp range: 50–55°F (10–13°C)
- Attenuation: 80–84%
- Flocculation: Medium
- Flavor: Clean, crisp
- Pressure tolerance: High (≤15 psi)

Preferred house strain for IPAs: Chico
Preferred house strain for Lagers: German Lager
