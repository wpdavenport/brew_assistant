Copper Crown ESB - BOS Day-of-Brew Tips

Use with copper_crown_brew_day_sheet.html. Goal: classic 11C balance, clean fermentation, no oxygen faults.
Critical Targets Numbers Fast Decision Rule
Pre-boil 7.2-7.4 gal (27.3-28.0 L), 1.042-1.044 If low gravity: extend boil. If high gravity: top up hot RO and re-check.
Post-boil (pre-chill) 5.4-5.6 gal (20.4-21.2 L), 1.056-1.058 Do not chase volume and gravity blindly; protect both together.
Into fermenter ~5.0 gal (19.0 L), OG 1.057, pitch 64.4 F (18.0 C) Single oxygen dose at pitch only (60-90 sec).

Mash In

Do: Dough in slowly and stir aggressively for the first 2-3 min to kill dry pockets.
Do: Keep recirculation steady, not blasting the bed.
Do: pH check at 10-15 min on a room-temp sample. Target 5.30.
Trap: Thin mash + hot pH reading can trick you into over-correcting.

Sparging / Runoff

System note: This recipe is full-volume, no-sparge on G40.
Do: Drain basket 8-12 min. Light press only if needed for flow, no hard squeezing.
Do: Keep 0.3-0.5 gal (1.1-1.9 L) hot RO ready for controlled top-up.
Trap: Over-extracting the bed can sharpen bitterness/harshness.

Rests

Main rest: 151.0 F (66.1 C) for 60 min.
Mash out: 168.0 F (75.6 C) for 10 min.
Do: Hold stable temp more tightly than +/-1 F if possible.
Trap: Long drift above target pushes sweeter finish for this strain.

Boil

Do: Vigorous, clean 60 min boil (not violent). Track boil-off rate.
Do: At 10 min, dissolve invert sugar in hot wort before full return.
Do: Keep hop timing exact; this style is about bitter precision, not hop saturation.
Trap: Late-addition drift or extra whirlpool time can unbalance style expression.

Whirlpool / Flameout

At 0: Flameout, add Northdown, steep 10 min.
Do: Gentle movement only; avoid whipping in air while hot.
Do: Respect the 10 min window. Longer can add rough edge.
Trap: Extended hot-side stand can blur bitterness quality.

Transfer and Pitch

Do: Chill fast to 64.4 F (18.0 C), then transfer clear wort only.
Do: Oxygenate once (60-90 sec), pitch decanted starter slurry, then stop oxygen.
Do: Record yeast generation (G0/G1+) and source batch if repitched.
Trap: Any post-pitch oxygen pickup will dull this beer quickly.
Fermentation gates: D4 ramp + gentle rouse, forced VDK pass before crash, FG stable 48 hr before package.
No crash or package until VDK is clean and terminal gravity is stable.
Closed transfer and low-oxygen packaging are non-negotiable for BOS quality.
