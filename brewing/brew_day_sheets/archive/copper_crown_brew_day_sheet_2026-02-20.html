Brew Notes

OG landed on target at 1.057. Flameout hop steep was held 10 minutes at 170F. Mash pH measured 5.3 and efficiency was 72%. Fermentation needed an aggressive day-3 intervention: ramp to 67F, then 68F, plus yeast rouse after butter was detected. Butter remained present on day 6, then cleared by day 9 before cold crash. Packaging FG came in higher than the earlier 1.014 Tilt observation, so keep both readings in mind when evaluating attenuation behavior on future batches.
